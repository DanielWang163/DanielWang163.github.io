Map: Dorado

Strategies

Tips

Any maps that involve reaching a point first and then holding it will benefit from having Lucio as their
Support Hero. While it's true that the objective doesn't unlock for a certain period of time, the first
team to get the point in one piece typically has a major advantage. Use that speed boost to get everyone
where they should be as quickly as possible. Junkrat is another character that becomes quite powerful on
Lijiang Tower. Being able to fling a whole load of explosives at the currently contested point provides a
powerful advantage. You might also consider taking at least one Mei with you in this game mode too - the
ability to throw a physical barrier here there and everywhere is a powerful one indeed. Symmetra's turrets
obviously bring something special to these kind of fights too. Regardless of the rest of your team
composition, a Pharah can be an incredibly powerful addition to your team on Lijiang Tower. There will be
plenty of opportunities to take to the skies and clatter your rocket fire into enemies, who will often have
little choice but to explore out of cover. Many buildings on this map also provide an extra entrance point
for Heroes who can reach high places too. You'll benefit of course from knowing the locations of the health
packs on all maps, but this goes double on Liiang Tower where firefights are often extremely intense. If
you can get your hands on a rejuvenating health boost, you'll significantly increase the odds of surviving
a one-on-one showdown. When you're fighting for the second capture point, be incredibly wary of the
bottomless pit that surrounds it. You will almost certainly take a few embarrassing tumbles into it during
a frantic firefight, but try to stay aware of this environmental danger at all times. The final control
point is a little more enclosed. If you need to find some breathing space, look for the stairwell that
takes you to a ledge that overlooks the actual control point. You may find yourself able to get in a kill
or two before you're spotted and - probably - eliminated.
