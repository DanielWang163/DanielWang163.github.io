Frag Launcher

Type: Arcing splash projectile
Damage: Direct hit: 120, Splash max: 80, Splash min: 12.5
Spread angle: Pinpoint
Projectile speed: 17.5 meters per second
Area of effect: 0.2 meters (projectile size)
Rate of fire: 1 shot per 0.6 seconds
Ammo: 5
Reload time: 1.5 seconds
Cannot Headshot
Description: Junkrat's Frag Launcher lobs grenades a significant distance. They bounce to reach their
destination, and blow up when they strike an enemy. The grenades explode after three bounces or when
they hit an enemy unit.

Concussion Mine

Type: Arcing projectile
Damage: Splash max: 120, Splash min: 35
Projectile speed: 17.5 meters per second
Area of effect: 3 meters radius
Ammo: 2 charges
Duration: Until detonated
Cooldown: 8 seconds for one charge, 16 seconds for both charges
Cannot Headshot
Description: After placing one of his homemade Concussion Mines, Junkrat can trigger it to damage enemies
and send them flying... or propel himself through the air. The mine does not deal self-damage, but still
applies the knockback, making it usable as a mobility tool.

Health: 100
Damage: 80
Area of effect: 1 meter radius
Duration: 1 second stun, 3 seconds root
Cooldown 10 seconds
Cannot Headshot

receives an audio cue when an enemy triggers the trap.
