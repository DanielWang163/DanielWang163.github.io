Recipe

Wonton

Ingredients

  • Raw Wonton
  • Cooking Pot
  • Water
  • Soy Sause
  • Shrimp
  • Seaweed
  • Monosodium Glutamate
  • Salt
  • White Pepper

This is a recipe for my secret homemade wonton.

Steps to Follow

  1. Put water in the cooking pan until the cooking pan is 1/4 full
  2. Wait until the water is boiled
  3. Put in the amount of wonton you would like to eat
  4. Wait until the water is boiled again
  5. While waiting, get a big bowl to make the sauce
  6. Put in other ingredients in this order
    • Salt
    • White pepper
    • Monosodium Glutamate
    • Shrimp
    • Seaweed
    • Soy sauce
  7. After the water is boiled again
  8. Pour everything from the cooking pan into the big bowl
  9. Finished
