Recipe

Wonton

Ingredients

  1. Raw Wonton
  2. Cooking Pot
  3. Water
  4. Soy Sause
  5. Shrimp
  6. Seaweed
  7. Monosodium Glutamate
  8. Salt
  9. White Pepper

Steps to Follow

  1. Put water in the cooking pan until the cooking pan is 1/4 full
  2. Wait until the water is boiled
  3. Put in the amount of wonton
  4. Wait until the water is boiled again
  5. While waiting, get a big bowl to make the sauce
  6. Put in other ingredients in this order
  7. Salt
  8. White pepper
  9. Monosodium Glutamate
  10. Shrimp
  11. Seaweed
  12. Soy sauce
  13. After the water is boiled again
  14. Pour everything in the cooking pan into the big bowl
  15. Finish
